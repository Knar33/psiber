Cairn

cairn [kairn]
noun
a heap of stones set up as a landmark, monument, tombstone, etc. (dictionary.com)

Summary

Cairn is a multiplayer virtual web experience where authenticated users can move and stack a collection of rocks on an island. The rocks will be locked in place after a period of time, forming a final piece of collaborative artwork.

Design

Cairn will be built as a [Hyperfy] world with a custom app
Users can authenticate by signing in with a wallet containing a Hyperfy World token.
Authenticated Users can click a stone and it will go into their hand if:

  1. there are no stones on top of it
  2. there are no users on top of it
  3. the user has no stone in their hand
Authenticated users with a stone in their hand can click to place the stone down at the cursor location if:
  1. the surface normal is facing upwards (you have to obey gravity)
  2. there are no users standing in the vicinity
Users that log out with a stone in their hand drop it on the ground

I will release a contract and airdrop free tokens to the last person that touched each stone, or send one to each of the top active users if there is a lot of activity.
The stones will be procedurally generated gltf files which I'm hoping I can do entirely on-chain. If I can't manage that I will generate them off-chain and release a static collection.
